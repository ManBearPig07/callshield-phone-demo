Start here

CallShield is not a generic AI receptionist. It is a paid-lead recovery proof layer for home-service businesses that buy inbound calls and need to know what booked, what leaked, and what required a person.

What pain it solves

Owners spend on calls but cannot always see which calls became booked jobs, which were out of area, which were wrong service, and which wasted staff time.

Why it is different

Receptionists answer calls. CallShield classifies paid-call outcomes, preserves risk escalation, and proves recovered gross profit.

Why home inspection first

Home inspection has clear geography, clear service scope, urgent buyer intent, and clean ROI math.

Why HVAC second

HVAC has the same paid-call leakage, plus higher ticket values and real trust objections like licensed technicians, stocked trucks, and floor protection.
